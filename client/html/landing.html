Did you just move to a new country? Did you have to run away in fear for your own life? Did you have to leave your whole world behind as a child? Was it because of war? Probably not. However, this is true for nearly 600,000 refugees from Ukraine who are now seeking refuge in Poland. They don't know the language, people and culture.

We have created SIEDU for you! We can't imagine what you are feeling right now, but we are here to help and to make the process of assimilation as quick and easy as possible. We will help you learn, find yourself in a new environment, we will show you Poland and Poles at their best. How? All you have to do is ask and we will help! Help knows no boundaries!

who?

A community of young and passionate students determined to help their peers from Ukraine.

why?

We want to help students and children from Ukraine to minimize the disruption of their education and their everyday lives.

how?

We help in assimilation through free, personalized education and events to help integrate the Polish and Ukrainian communities.
