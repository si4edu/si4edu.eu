Who are we?

The Student Initiative for Education (SIEDU) was launched on March 5th, 2022 by a student (surprise!).
Today, there are dozens of brilliant, very nice, and energetic people working in its ranks
  • for one purpose: to help peers in need
  • in two ways: individual lessons and integration events
  • for one reason: empathy

SIEDU is a NON-PROFIT organization: what we do, we do from the heart.
We are politically neutral, we operate in accordance with humanitarian values and the applicable law.
Registration of the entity "Fundacja SIEDU" is in progress.
